SHEILD has been an expert in guiding budding entrepreneurs and helping turn their ideas into a startup with an immaculate business model.

As a collaboration between Venturesity VIT and SHEILD, HackOverflow brings together the best of both worlds into a technopreneurial hackathon, a first-of-its-kind for Vellore Institute of Technology.

At HackOverflow, we plan on judging students not only on the basis of the technical aspects of their ideas, but give equal weightage to the managerial aspects. Hello World 2.0

Hello World 2.0 is a pre-gravitas technical event organized for the freshers to introduce them to the world of hacks and technical answers to real world problems.

In this hackathon participants are given the task to create products that would solve problems in chosen few fields like - Healthcare, Security, Digital India, Education, and Connecting India with respect to the Gravitas theme "Technology for Non-technologists."

The duration of the hackathon is 36 hours, which includes team formation, ideation, product/prototype development, and final pitch. The participants would be judged on the basis of the following criterion - Originality, Presentation, Product Quality, Code Quality and Final Pitch.

About

Getting to know people is hard, because humans aren’t one-dimensional. We are a technological innovation chapter with a little bit of goofiness, all amalgamated into a bunch of crazy workaholics, who also work for the benefit of individuals looking for meaningful experiences, as well as the enhancement of technical literacy. We predominantly operate on coffee and code. If you aren’t impressed yet, we would expect you to leave this page without reading our awesome content.

In the course of a year, we organize various events, including hackathons, like Hello World, and HackOverflow. The workshops conducted by the chapter have had positive outcomes till date, and we strive to use our experience to create results that everyone is insanely proud of. Passion is contagious and many of us naturally prefer to work with people who love what they do.

You could believe we are a boring, self-serving mix of ‘me’ ‘me’ ‘me’, and ‘us’ ‘us’ ‘us’, but before you start to take us seriously, we should let you know, sharing ideas and personal experiences are what form the foundation of this small family.
