Hello World

We conducted our flagship hackathon Hello World during Gravitas'17, VIT's annual technical fest. The main purpose of Hello World was to

introduce the freshers to the world of hacks and making them realise the true potential of themselves as well as their competent. Problem statements involved Heathcare, Security, Digital India, Education and Connecting India.

It was a 24-hour hackathon including separate sessions for team formation, idea presentation, product/prototype development and final pitch. Participants were judged on the basis of the following criterion. Originality, Presentation, Product quality, Code quality and Final presentation. Hello World 2.0

Hello World 2.0 is a pre-gravitas technical event organized for the freshers to introduce them to the world of hacks and technical answers to real world problems.

In this hackathon participants are given the task to create products that would solve problems in chosen few fields like - Healthcare, Security, Digital India, Education, and Connecting India with respect to the Gravitas theme "Technology for Non-technologists."

The duration of the hackathon is 36 hours, which includes team formation, ideation, product/prototype development, and final pitch. The participants would be judged on the basis of the following criterion - Originality, Presentation, Product Quality, Code Quality and Final Pitch.
